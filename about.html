About Neo Entangle X

A glimpse into the minds behind the teleportation revolution.

NEO-ENTANGLE.X is a visionary research initiative led by Jaideep and his talented team of innovators. The project explores the cutting edge of quantum teleportation and IoT-driven robotics, aiming to merge theoretical physics with real-world engineering applications.

What started as a campus innovation is now a movement powered by deep curiosity and futuristic ambition. The team is committed to developing experimental models that challenge traditional boundaries in communication, control systems, and automation — all built on quantum principles.

Whether you’re a student, developer, or enthusiast, Neo Entangle X invites you to join the journey toward tomorrow’s technology — today.
